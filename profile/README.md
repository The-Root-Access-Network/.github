# 🌍 The Root Access Network (TRAN)

> *"I am because we are."*

**TRAN** is a Lagos-based cybersecurity education company dedicated to making digital safety knowledge accessible to everyone — from secondary school students to early-career professionals across Africa and beyond.

---

## 🔐 What We Do

| Initiative | Description |
|---|---|
| 🏫 **Cybersecurity Educational School Tour (CEST)** | Taking cybersecurity awareness into secondary schools across Lagos — with Kahoot games, live demos, and real conversations |
| 🤝 **Ubuntu Bridge Initiative** | Weekly data support for learners across multiple countries who can't afford subscriptions, plus monthly laptop powerbank giveaways |
| 🎲 **CYTRAC** | Our flagship cybersecurity board game that teaches practical digital safety skills through play |
| 🎙️ **Monthly Webinar Series** | Free, practical sessions on cybersecurity careers, software engineering, CV readiness, and industry prep |
| 🎤 **Street Vox-Pop Interviews** | Raising public cybersecurity awareness through community conversations |

---
